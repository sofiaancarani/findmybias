A bias is basically your favorite member within a K-pop group. In other words the person who stands out to you or who you simply become attached to. Sometimes a K-pop fan even develops an ultimate bias, who is their favorite member not only among a single group but among all K-pop groups.

K-pop groups tend to be very numerous (some even have more than 20 members!) and because of that it could be very difficult to find your favourite one among all of them. By answering a few questions of these quizzes we will tell you which member of the group you are most likely to be compatible with.
