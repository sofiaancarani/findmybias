A bias is basically a favorite member within a K-pop group. In other words the person who stands out to you or who you simply become attached to. Fans may sometimes even develop an ultimate bias, who is their favorite not only among a single group but among all K-pop idol groups.

K-pop groups tend to be very numerous (some groups even have more than 20 memebers!!!) and because of that it could be very difficult to find a bias. By answering a few questions of these quizzes we will tell you which memeber of the group you are most likely to be compatible with.
